Governance model

Hands-off does not mean hidden. The UI should make the autonomy contract inspectable and adjustable per project, repo, and ticket.

Product rules

What the UI should expose

  • Current autonomy level and exactly what it permits.
  • Why a ticket advanced without confirmation.
  • Why a ticket stopped before agent execution.
  • Evidence bundle completeness for every transition.
  • Project-level pause and per-ticket override controls.

What should stay out of the way

  • Full logs during healthy routine work.
  • Raw agent transcript as the default evidence view.
  • Dense board columns when there are no decisions to make.
  • Celebratory status language for routine automation.
  • Global alerts for isolated ticket-level issues.
